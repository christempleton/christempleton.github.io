Join our team!

Are you interested in studying animal communication, cognition,  behavioural ecology, or conservation? We are always excited to have motivated and engaged students of all levels join our research group.

My goal in the lab is to foster a collaborative research environment, with students working closely together and everyone pitching in and helping with all of the projects. We work hard but also have a lot of fun times in the lab and through extracurricular activities (for example, our annual trip to pick berries shown to the right!).

Pacific undergraduates interested in gaining research experience during the summer or school year should contact me directly via email. We have lots of different opportunities for undergrads of all levels to learn new techniques in acoustic analysis, help with field and lab work, or develop their own capstone thesis research projects.
